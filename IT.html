Department of Information Technology


The Department of Information Technology (IT) is a center of excellence providing in-depth technical knowledge and opportunities for innovation and research. The department is progressing by continual improvement in standards of teaching with the help of qualified and efficient staff. Department has accomplished project- based learning by adopting and developing projects from diversified areas of society.

Exclusive and high performance computing laboratories with necessary software's and high speed internet facility. Department possesses highly resourceful library with titles on varied disciplines of Information Technology.

Consistently good results with an average of more than 90% at B.E. Examination with many students securing distinction. Some of our major recruiters are TCS, Capgemini, Mastek, eClerx, LTI, Relience Joi, NSEIT to name the few. Strong industry interface with leading companies like TCS and Mastek.

Departmental Advisory Board in all ways contributes in planning and executing academic session. Additional efforts to improve performance of the students are provided through remedial lectures. Team of academic co-coordinator, class In charges, student Teacher guardians extend all possible support to the students to enhance their capabilities and performance. The effort and support are not only for academics but also for overall personality development along with extra-curricular and co-curricular activities which includes hands-on workshops by expertise, technical talks.

Group of Information Technology (GITS)a student association actively organizes various technical & non-technical events which helps in their overall development.. Faculty members represent the Mumbai University on various authorities such as Faculty of Technology, Board of Studies and Advisory Board of various colleges.
