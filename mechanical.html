Department of Mechanical Engineering


The Department of Mechanical engineering is established in 1993 for the under graduate course. Masters in Mechanical Engineering with specialization in CAD/CAM and Robotics was introduced in 2011. The department has approved Research Center for Ph.D. program recognized by the University of Mumbai.

The department has well equipped laboratories and in-house computer center with 3D modeling and computational software such as AUTOCAD, IDEA, ANSYS, CREO, Lab View, and Visual Basic. The department has well-equipped workshop with advanced machines like CNC Turning and Milling. Various sponsored projects from professional institutions like ISHRAE are undertaken for indigenous development of the test rigs facilities. Apart from central library, the departmental library is having more than 600 reference books for the students and faculty.

Departmental student chapters include ISHRAE (Indian society of Heating, refrigerating and air conditioning Engineers), ASHRAE (American society of Heating, refrigerating and air conditioning Engineers) and SAE (Society of Automotive Engineers) and IIIE (Indian Institution of Industrial Engineering). Students organization the Forum of Mechanical Engineering Students (FOMES) regularly conducts various technical events.

The experienced faculty and staff of the department made remarkable strides by involving themselves in academic activities like conducting various Faculty development program, Workshops, Seminars and Industry- Interaction programs. Faculties have grabbed the research grants from the University of Mumbai.

The department of Mechanical Engineering has MOUs with leading & reputed industries like RCF and CAD Center. The department has professional body membership with ISTE and IIIE. Students have also qualified many national and international level examinations such as GATE and GRE. Students are encouraged and participated actively in various sports & cultural activities together with some important technical events. The department alumni students are extending guidance and support in guest lectures. The students of mechanical have placed in many reputed companies. RESO
